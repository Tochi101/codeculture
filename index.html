Why Choose Us

Experienced Staffs

We at Code-Culture, are a team of experts which train and develop young minds in information and communication technology, most especially programming (coding). We are experienced in child management and we would love to offer a proposal to teach students at your educational institution. Our tech program offers to teach pupils how to code using scratch programming language, which is suitable for kids from age (8-14). It is a creative and fun filled experience for each child that participates. It is easy to understand and children can learn to code, with little or no prior technological knowledge.
